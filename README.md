## About

Apparely is an AI-powered fashion assistant that helps you catalog your wardrobe and get personalized outfit recommendations. Take photos of your clothes, chat with Lumina (your AI stylist), and never wonder what to wear again.

## Features

- **Snap Your Wardrobe** — AI auto-detects, crops, and catalogs clothing items
- **Chat with Lumina** — Natural conversation flow for personalized recommendations
- **Swipe to Decide** — Tinder-style outfit browsing that learns your taste
- **Wardrobe Health Score** — Insights into variety, color balance, and wear frequency
- **Privacy First** — On-device image analysis keeps your photos local

## Tech Stack

- **Mobile**: Flutter (iOS)
- **AI/ML**: CoreML, Apple Vision, on-device heuristics
- **Backend**: Firebase Functions (TypeScript)
- **Subscriptions**: RevenueCat
- **Showcase Site**: Next.js, React Three Fiber, Framer Motion, Tailwind CSS
